Event: Indradhanush - An Essay Competition
A competition that encourages and helps one widen their literary boundaries and put to use their writing skills for a greater cause.

Rules:
Each category has been given a different topic to write an essay on.
Please choose your category based on the standard (grade) you are in and follow the rules of that category.
If you do not fit in any of the mentioned categories, you can still choose to compete in our open category.
You can choose to write your essay in English or in Hindi
Plaigarism of any form is not allowed. This includes- copying words or ideas from the internet,copy-pasting information from any website, changing words but copying the sentence from a source.
