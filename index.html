Aparigraha Foundation is a nonprofit organization that is created to educate and empower citizens of India of all ages- Beyond religion, culture, or any other discrimination. It is inspired by UNESCO’s 17 SDG’s. Our strong-willed staff, driven donors, and generous international volunteers provide the innovation, originality, and resources needed to empower these sections of our society.
