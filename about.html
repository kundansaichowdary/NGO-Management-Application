ABOUT US

Mission Statement

Who are we?
Aparigraha Foundation E - 36240(M) is a nonprofit organization registered under the Charity Commission of Greater Mumbai . It is created to educate, empower citizens of India of all ages; beyond religion, culture or any other discrimination; inspired by UNESCO’s 17 SDG’s. Our strong-willed staff, driven donors and generous group of international volunteers provide the innovation, originality, and resources needed to empower these sections of our society.Our strong-willed staff, driven donors and generous group of international volunteers provide the innovation, originality, and resources needed to empower these sections of our society.

What we value
Aparigraha Foundation is based on 3 A's of Lord Mahavira-

Non-Violence (Ahimsa) is compassion and forgiveness in thoughts, words, and deeds towards all living beings.
Non-Absolutism (Anekantvad) is respecting the views of others. Jains encourage dialog and harmony with other faiths.
Non-Possessiveness (Aparigraha) is the balancing of needs and desires, while staying detached from our possessions.
We, at Aparigraha Foundation believe that the best way to live life is to ‘Live and Let Live’.

Our Vision

To build entrepreneurs who make a significant difference in the quality of their own lives, the society, the community, the nation and the universe while conserving the environment.

Our History

What began nearly twenty five years ago as a girl’s vision to offer education to few children has grown into the APARIGRAHA Foundation. Documented on 23rd September, 2021, APARIGRAHA Foundation was built upon the belief system that regardless of stature, each individual intellect has the power to explore its unique skills and abilities to experience satisfaction in whatever one pursues for in a well honoured and respectable living. Associating, influencing and exemplifying true innovation throughout the world, the Foundation provides an impact through a coalition of resilient and thoughtful volunteers and donors.

Goals

Aparigraha Foundation implements the UNESCO SDG - 17 Goals to Transform Our World.

We are dedicated to creating skill based, environment friendly learning that empowers Gen Alpha, Gen Z, and people of all ages across the pyramid of the population, destitute and the imprisoned population, all who need innovative entrepreneurial skills to create a self sustaining and independent way of living.
